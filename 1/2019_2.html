Барбер из гетто
| В чем твоя суперсила?



Kana in collaboration with AXE created a series of shorts about young people who followed their passion regardless of the stereotypes imposed by the society - it’s a known fact that since childhood many are influenced to choose a different path. However, some are lucky to live their dreams and despite opinions of those around them stay true to their calling.

Tair is the subject of our first episode. Tair left his hometown Shymkent and came to Almaty where he started career as a hairdresser. For a long time he was just a hair technician in a local salon. Eventually, he was inspired by the western barbershop culture and started his own, the very first barbershop in Kazakhstan. At first, Tair had to explain his clients what the barbershop values and purposes are. Gradually, the new men’s grooming culture won over hearts of many Kazakhstanis.

He opened studios across the country and abroad, trained 600 barbers and is actively involved in charitable work. He made a career choice that was considered unusual at the time, but he believed in himself and proved that having ambition and hunger for work is enough in succeeding and becoming the hero of your own life.
